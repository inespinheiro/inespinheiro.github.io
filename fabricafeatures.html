Fabrica Features

Management / Visual Communication
(2019 - 2020)

With Vítor Serra

Fabrica Features is a gallery/concept store in Lisbon that operates under Fabrica — the communications research centre in Treviso, Italy financed by the Benetton Group. It is defined by the attitudes and creations of the young international people who work there, refreshed by the coming and going of studio members.
